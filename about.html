Our Story

Building Trust Through Excellence

Amoudi & Associates CPAs was founded on the principle that quality accounting services should be accessible, transparent, and tailored to each client's unique needs. With years of experience in tax preparation, bookkeeping, and business accounting, we've established ourselves as a trusted partner for individuals and small businesses across the nation. While our office is in Downers Grove, IL, we serve clients in all 50 states through phone, video call, and our secure document portal.

Our mission is simple: to help our clients achieve their financial goals through strategic planning, expert guidance, and meticulous attention to detail. We believe that great CPA services go beyond compliance - they provide insights that help you make better financial decisions and grow your business.

Whether you're a busy professional, a growing small business, or an established company, we're committed to providing personalized service that makes a real difference in your financial success.
