Frequently Asked Questions

How can a CPA help my small business save money?

+

A CPA goes beyond basic tax filing. We analyze your business structure, identify overlooked deductions, implement tax-efficient strategies, and provide proactive planning throughout the year. Many small business owners save significantly more than the cost of our services through reduced tax liability and smarter financial decisions.

Do I need bookkeeping if I'm a small business?

+

Yes. Accurate bookkeeping is essential for every small business, regardless of size. It helps you track income and expenses, prepare for tax season, monitor cash flow, and make informed business decisions. Clean books also make it easier to secure loans, attract investors, and stay compliant with tax regulations.

Should my business be an LLC or S-Corp?

+

The best entity structure depends on your specific situation, including your income level, number of owners, and growth plans. An S-Corp can offer significant self-employment tax savings for qualifying businesses, while an LLC provides simplicity and flexibility. Our CPAs will evaluate your circumstances and recommend the structure that minimizes your tax burden. Call us at (331) 264-4163 for a free consultation.
