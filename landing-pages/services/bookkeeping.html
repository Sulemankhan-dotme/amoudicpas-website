Frequently Asked Questions

What information do I need to provide for bookkeeping?

+

We'll need access to your business bank and credit card statements, invoices, receipts, and any other financial records. Many clients provide this through online banking portals or email, and we can also arrange document pickup. We'll discuss the most convenient method for your business during your consultation.

How often will you update my books?

+

Most of our clients receive monthly bookkeeping updates. Transactions are entered, reconciled, and categorized monthly so your books stay current. For high-volume businesses, we can arrange more frequent updates. We'll determine the best schedule for your specific needs during your consultation.

Can you work with the accounting software my business already uses?

+

Yes! We work with most popular accounting software including QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks, Wave, and others. If you don't have software yet, we can recommend the best solution for your business size and needs. We'll handle the setup and management so you don't have to.

What is the cost of your bookkeeping services?

+

Bookkeeping costs depend on factors like transaction volume, number of accounts, and complexity of your business. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Most small businesses find our services more cost-effective than hiring an in-house bookkeeper. Contact us at (331) 264-4163 for a customized quote.
