Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to bring?

+

Common documents include: W-2s from employers, 1099 forms (1099-INT, 1099-DIV, 1099-MISC, etc.), rental income statements, business records, mortgage interest statements, charitable donation receipts, and medical expense records. We'll provide a complete checklist during your consultation.

How much does tax preparation cost?

+

Our pricing depends on the complexity of your return. Simple returns start at affordable rates, while complex returns with multiple income sources are priced accordingly. We provide transparent pricing upfront with no hidden fees. Call us at (331) 264-4163 for a quote specific to your situation.

Can you help with back taxes or prior-year returns?

+

Yes! We specialize in preparing prior-year returns and can help you get current with the IRS. Whether you need to file amendments or catch up on multiple years, our CPAs have the expertise to handle your situation professionally and minimize any penalties.

What if I get audited?

+

If the IRS contacts you about your return, we can represent you in the audit process. Our meticulous preparation and documentation support make audits straightforward. We stand behind our work and will help you through every step.
