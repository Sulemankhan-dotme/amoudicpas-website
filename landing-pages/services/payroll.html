Frequently Asked Questions

How often can we process payroll?

+

We can process payroll on virtually any schedule that works for your business - weekly, bi-weekly, semi-monthly, or monthly. Most businesses use weekly or bi-weekly payroll, but we're flexible based on your preferences and employee needs. We can discuss the best option for your business during your consultation.

What if we have employees in multiple states?

+

No problem! We handle multi-state payroll with expertise. Different states have different tax rules, and we ensure compliance with each state's requirements. Whether you have employees in Illinois, neighboring states, or across the country, we can manage it seamlessly.

What information do you need from us each pay period?

+

Simply provide us with employee hours worked (or salary information if salaried) and any special deductions or changes. You can submit this through our secure online portal, email, or phone call. We handle all the calculations, tax withholdings, and deductions from there.

How much does payroll processing cost?

+

Payroll processing costs depend on factors like number of employees, frequency of pay periods, and additional services needed. Most businesses find outsourcing payroll more cost-effective than hiring staff to handle it in-house. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees.
